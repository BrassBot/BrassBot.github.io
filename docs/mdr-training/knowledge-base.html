MDR Services

Our team of medical doctors are specialists at building the clinical evidence portfolio that you need for MDR compliance of your products.

  • We will design, implement, and maintain your MDR compliant Post Market Surveillance systems from scratch or update your MDD system for the new regulations.

    Learn more about our Post-Market Surveillance (PMS) service

    • Designing your PMS system
    • Vigilance systems
    • Medical complaints
    • Writing PMS plans & PSURs
    • Clinical investigations & product registries
    • Risk management
    • Identify needs for any PACAs / FSCAs

  • We will develop your PMCF strategy to allow continuous collection and evaluation of clinical data of your medical devices in order to produce real world evidence.

    Learn more about our Post-Market Clinical Follow-up (PMCF) service

    • Design your PMCF strategy
    • Develop compliant RWE generation systems
    • Ensure complete lifecycle evidence generation
    • Prepare evidence reports for other departments
    • Reduce time waste on administration

  • Our full Clinical Evaluation solution involves a 360-degree analysis of your medical devices, writing and reviewing CERs and designing clinical development plans.

    Learn more about our Clinical Evaluation service

    • Performing 360-degree clinical evaluations
    • Write & review CERs
    • Systematic literature appraisals
    • Clinical evidence gap analyses
    • Clinical development plans
    • Designing & perform clinical investigations

  • Our medics will apply General Safety and Performance Requirements to your medical devices, to determine whether any gaps exist in your current evidence portfolio.

    Learn more about our Gap Analysis service

    • Identify your products general SPRs
    • Find gaps in existing evidence portfolio
    • Systematic literature reviews
    • Clinical development plans
    • Impact analysis on risk management processes
