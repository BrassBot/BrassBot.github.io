Split The Bills

Split the Bills is a Sheffield based company that takes the fuss out of shared student utility bills.

I've been their freelance Front-end Developer since 2013 and worked with their Design and Dev teams on many of their projects. I have also done Interaction Design work for them over the years, creating a vision for their user account centre.

Their web presence is predominantly run through a Ruby on Rails application, but I have also been involved with integrating their brand on their help & support site (powered by Desk) and also creating custom Wordpress themes for the careers and news sites.
